Getting Started

There are a multitude of errors and fascinating configuration issues that crop up on a regular basis here, so this wiki is for helping to keep track of the most common issues and recording their fixes. This should be considered a living document -- if you would like to add to it, make sure that you have access to the root repo here or send the info to a Kenzie staff member who does have edit access. We'll do our best to get that info added in a timely manner.
